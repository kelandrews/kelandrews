What is Ever-Save ?

EVER-SAVE is a pioneering program by Kelandrews Energy Solutions (K.E.S), using advanced technologies to improve socio-economic conditions. Its goal is to enhance living conditions by tackling various contemporary challenges. EVER-SAVE is a multifaceted initiative that combines innovation, education, employment, and environmental stewardship. The prog​ram aims not only to transform individual lives but also to create a positive ripple effect in communities, the nation, and Africa as a whole.

ENERGY CONSUMPTION CUT-DOWN

A cornerstone of the EVER-SAVE program is its pledge to alleviate the financial strain of fuel & electricity consumption. Leveraging state-of-the-art technologies, EVER-SAVE achieves a 30% reduction in costs.

Why Ever-Save ?

1.    Economic Relief

Significant reduction (30%) in fuel and electricity costs, providing financial relief for households and businesses, redirecting funds to other essential needs, fostering economic stability and resilience.

2.    Health Empowerment

Community education on the hidden dangers of EMR from electronic devices and provision of practical solutions and protective measures to safeguard individuals and families. This improved public health outcomes through proactive measures against potential health consequences.

3.    Job Opportunities

Robust job creation initiatives, inclusive recruitment strategies targeting the youth, working class, and pensioners. This improves livelihood of people as they find for themselves a new stream of making income by being pioneers for the Ever-Save Program

4.    Life & Property Protection

The program emphasizes compliance with Electrical Wiring regulations, which can significantly enhance the safety of electrical installations. This can prevent electrical fires, shocks, and other hazards related to noncompliance with safety standards.

5.    ​Societal Benefit

Ever-Save, driven by cutting-edge technologies, contributes to technological innovation in the energy sector. National efficiency in fossil fuel usage. This not only enhances the overall technological landscape of the society but also positions the community as a forward-thinking hub for sustainable solutions.

6.    Environmental Conservation

Integration of negative ions in technologies to reduce carbon monoxide and pollutants emissions and contribution to global sustainability goals and addressing specific environmental challenges in Ghana/Africa. In order to create a safer and cleaner environment for present and future generations.

Main Benefits of The Energy Saving Module

CONSUMPTION REDUCTION

Reduces fuel & electricty consumption by 30% or even more. It works on fuel types and all vehicles and all homes or electricity consumers.

PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATION

Boosts engine performance, smoothens engine running & extends lifespan. As the fuel is ionnized by infrared and negative ions and makes it burn easier.

EMISSIONS REDUCTION

Prevents incomplete combustion in motor vehicles which lead to the formation of carbon monoxide gas, which is a health & environmental  hazard.

PROTECTION OF ELECTRONIC DEVICES 

As a special "current stablizer" the installation is able to minimize possible damages by fluctuating currents, heating and radiation losses in electrical system.
